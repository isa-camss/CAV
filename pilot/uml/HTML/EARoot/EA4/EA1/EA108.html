The Agent that is legally responsible for the given use of the Channel.<br/><br/>Additional Information<br/>This ownership does not refer to the agent that manages the channel (that could be a service provider, too) but to the one that is legally responsible or accountable for the service being provided through it. For example the buyer that is offering a channel to Economic Operators for one specific purpose, like the provision of information. <br/><br/>
